Basic Digital Safety

There are many quick and easy ways to safeguard yourself from digital threats, here is a list of all of the simplest ways:

-Checking the url

If there is a lock to the right of the url, it is a good sign that the website is safe.

-Spelling mistakes

Frequent spelling mistakes are a sure sign of nefarious intentions behind an email.

-VPNs

Using a VPN when accesing sites that you do not trust prevents them from stealing your ip details.

How does messing up affect you?

If a person with nefarious intentions gets their grubby little hands on your data, they can do with it.
ranging from tracking what websites you frequent, to fully impersonating you and deducting money from your bank account, these dangers to your wellbeing should be taken extremely seriously.
